FAQ

Common questions

What exactly is a "beta tester"?
You get free access to PriceWatch for 8 weeks before it launches publicly. In return, we ask for honest feedback — what works, what's confusing, what you wish it did. Your input directly shapes the final product. You also get a 50% lifetime discount when we launch paid plans.
How often does PriceWatch check prices?
In the MVP, prices are checked every 2–6 hours depending on your plan. We're working on more frequent checks for the Growth and Enterprise plans. You'll be alerted as soon as a change is detected in the next check cycle.
Does this work for Meesho and Shopify sellers too?
Amazon India and Flipkart are fully supported in beta. Meesho and Shopify support is being built during the beta period — beta testers get early access to these as soon as they're ready.
Is this legal? Will my Amazon account be affected?
Absolutely. PriceWatch only monitors publicly visible prices — the same prices any customer sees when browsing. We never access your seller account, Amazon Seller Central, or any private data. Your seller account is completely unaffected.
What happens after the 8-week free beta?
You'll move to a paid plan at a 50% founding discount — locked in for as long as you remain a customer. We'll give you at least 2 weeks notice before any billing starts, and you can cancel anytime with no questions asked.
Who is building this?
PriceWatch is built by LucideCore IT Solution, based in Ghaziabad, UP. We've built 150+ digital products for Indian businesses. This is our first SaaS product and we're building it specifically for Indian e-commerce sellers.
